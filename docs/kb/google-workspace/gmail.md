# Gmail

Gmail is Google's email service that provides a professional email solution with your custom domain.

## Overview

Gmail for Google Workspace offers a powerful email platform with advanced features, generous storage, and seamless integration with other Google Workspace services.

## Key Features

- **Custom Email Addresses** - Use your domain name (e.g., yourname@yourdomain.com)
- **Generous Storage** - Ample mailbox storage space
- **Powerful Search** - Find emails quickly with Google's search technology
- **Spam Protection** - Advanced spam filtering and security
- **Mobile Access** - Access your email from any device
- **Labels and Filters** - Organize your inbox efficiently
- **Integration** - Works seamlessly with Google Drive, Calendar, and other Workspace apps

## Accessing Gmail

You can access Gmail through:
- **Web Browser** - Visit [mail.google.com](https://mail.google.com)
- **Mobile Apps** - Download Gmail app for iOS or Android
- **Email Clients** - Configure IMAP or POP3 in your preferred email client

## Common Tasks

### Sending Email
1. Click "Compose" in Gmail
2. Enter the recipient's email address
3. Add a subject and message
4. Click "Send"

### Managing Your Inbox
- Use labels to organize emails
- Create filters to automatically sort incoming mail
- Archive emails to keep your inbox clean
- Use the search bar to find specific emails

### Security Best Practices
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Review and update your security settings regularly
- Be cautious with suspicious emails
- Use strong, unique passwords
