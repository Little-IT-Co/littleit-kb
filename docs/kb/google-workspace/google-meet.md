# Google Meet

Google Meet is a video conferencing solution that enables teams to connect through high-quality video meetings.

## Overview

Google Meet provides secure, reliable video meetings for teams of all sizes. It integrates seamlessly with Google Workspace and Google Calendar.

## Key Features

- **HD Video and Audio** - High-quality video and audio calls
- **Screen Sharing** - Share your screen or specific windows
- **Recording** - Record meetings for later review
- **Large Meetings** - Support for meetings with many participants
- **Calendar Integration** - Schedule meetings directly from Google Calendar
- **Mobile Access** - Join meetings from mobile devices
- **Security** - Secure meetings with encryption and access controls

## Starting a Meeting

### From Google Calendar
1. Create a new calendar event
2. Click "Add Google Meet video conferencing"
3. Share the meeting link with participants

### From Gmail
1. Open Gmail
2. Click "New meeting" in the sidebar
3. Copy the meeting link to share

### From meet.google.com
1. Go to [meet.google.com](https://meet.google.com)
2. Click "New meeting"
3. Choose "Start an instant meeting" or "Schedule in Google Calendar"

## Joining a Meeting

- **Via Link** - Click the meeting link shared with you
- **Via Calendar** - Click "Join with Google Meet" in your calendar event
- **Via Meeting Code** - Go to meet.google.com and enter the meeting code

## Meeting Features

### During a Meeting
- **Mute/Unmute** - Control your microphone
- **Camera On/Off** - Toggle your video
- **Screen Share** - Share your entire screen or a specific window
- **Chat** - Send messages during the meeting
- **Participants** - View who's in the meeting
- **Record** - Record the meeting (requires host permission)

### Host Controls
- **Admit Participants** - Control who can join the meeting
- **Mute Participants** - Mute all participants if needed
- **Remove Participants** - Remove participants from the meeting
- **End Meeting** - End the meeting for all participants

## Best Practices

- **Prepare in Advance** - Test your camera and microphone before joining
- **Use a Headset** - Reduce background noise with a headset
- **Check Your Background** - Ensure a professional or blurred background
- **Mute When Not Speaking** - Reduce background noise
- **Share Agenda** - Share meeting agenda in advance
- **Record Important Meetings** - Record meetings for team members who can't attend

## Security Tips

- Use meeting codes and links securely
- Enable "Only people in your organization can join" for internal meetings
- Don't share meeting links publicly
- Use waiting rooms for added security
- Review participants list regularly
