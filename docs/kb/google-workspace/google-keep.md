# Google Keep

Google Keep is a note-taking service that helps you capture ideas, create to-do lists, and organize information.

## Overview

Google Keep is a simple, user-friendly note-taking app that integrates with Google Workspace, making it easy to capture and organize your thoughts and tasks.

## Key Features

- **Quick Notes** - Capture ideas instantly
- **Lists** - Create to-do lists and checklists
- **Reminders** - Set time and location-based reminders
- **Labels** - Organize notes with labels
- **Color Coding** - Use colors to categorize notes
- **Image Support** - Add images to your notes
- **Voice Notes** - Record voice memos
- **Collaboration** - Share notes with team members
- **Mobile Access** - Access from any device

## Creating Notes

### Text Notes
1. Go to [keep.google.com](https://keep.google.com) or open the Keep app
2. Click "Take a note" or tap the plus icon
3. Start typing your note
4. Click outside the note to save automatically

### Lists
1. Click "New list" or use the list icon
2. Add items to your list
3. Check off items as you complete them

### Voice Notes
1. Click the microphone icon
2. Speak your note
3. Google Keep will transcribe it automatically

## Organizing Notes

### Labels
- Create labels to categorize notes
- Assign multiple labels to a note
- Filter notes by label

### Colors
- Assign colors to notes for visual organization
- Click the color palette icon on any note

### Pinning
- Pin important notes to the top
- Click the pin icon on any note

### Archiving
- Archive notes you no longer need
- Access archived notes from the menu

## Collaboration

- **Share Notes** - Click the collaborator icon to share with others
- **Set Permissions** - Control who can view or edit
- **Real-time Updates** - See changes as collaborators edit

## Reminders

### Time-based Reminders
1. Click the reminder icon on a note
2. Set date and time
3. Get notified when the reminder is due

### Location-based Reminders
1. Click the reminder icon
2. Select "Pick place"
3. Get reminded when you're at that location

## Mobile Access

Download the Google Keep app for:
- **iOS** - Available on the App Store
- **Android** - Available on Google Play

## Integration with Google Workspace

- **Google Docs** - Copy Keep notes directly into Google Docs
- **Google Calendar** - Notes with reminders appear in Calendar
- **Gmail** - Access Keep notes from Gmail sidebar

## Best Practices

- Use labels consistently for better organization
- Color-code notes by project or priority
- Set reminders for time-sensitive tasks
- Archive completed notes to keep your workspace clean
- Share notes with team members for collaboration
- Use voice notes for quick capture on the go
