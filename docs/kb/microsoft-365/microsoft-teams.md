# Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is a collaboration platform that combines chat, meetings, file sharing, and app integration in one place.

## Overview

Microsoft Teams helps teams stay connected and productive with persistent chat, video meetings, file collaboration, and integration with other Microsoft 365 services.

## Key Features

- **Chat** - Instant messaging and group conversations
- **Meetings** - HD video and audio meetings
- **File Sharing** - Share and collaborate on files
- **Channels** - Organize conversations by topic or project
- **App Integration** - Integrate with hundreds of apps
- **Screen Sharing** - Share your screen during meetings
- **Recording** - Record meetings for later review
- **Mobile Access** - Access Teams from mobile devices

## Getting Started

### Accessing Teams
- **Web App** - [teams.microsoft.com](https://teams.microsoft.com)
- **Desktop App** - Download Teams client for Windows or Mac
- **Mobile App** - Download Teams app for iOS or Android

### Sign In
1. Open Teams
2. Sign in with your Microsoft 365 credentials
3. Start chatting and collaborating

## Teams and Channels

### Teams
Teams are collections of people, conversations, and files around a project or topic.

### Creating a Team
1. Click **Teams** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Join or create a team**
3. Choose **Create team**
4. Select team type (Private, Public, or Org-wide)
5. Add team name and description
6. Add members

### Channels
Channels are where conversations and files are organized within a team.

### Creating a Channel
1. Select your team
2. Click **More options** â†’ **Add channel**
3. Enter channel name and description
4. Choose privacy (Standard or Private)

## Chat

### One-on-One Chat
- Click **Chat** in the left sidebar
- Click **New chat**
- Type the person's name
- Start chatting

### Group Chat
- Start a new chat
- Add multiple people
- Create a group conversation

### Chat Features
- **Formatting** - Format text with bold, italic, etc.
- **Emojis and GIFs** - Add emojis and GIFs
- **Files** - Share files in chat
- **Reactions** - React to messages
- **Mentions** - @mention people to get their attention

## Meetings

### Scheduling Meetings
1. Click **Calendar** in Teams
2. Click **New meeting**
3. Add meeting details
4. Add attendees
5. Click **Send**

### Joining Meetings
- Click meeting link in calendar
- Join from Teams app
- Join from email invitation
- Join via phone (audio only)

### During Meetings
- **Video/Audio** - Toggle camera and microphone
- **Screen Share** - Share your screen or specific windows
- **Chat** - Send messages during meeting
- **Participants** - View and manage participants
- **Recording** - Record the meeting (if enabled)

### Meeting Features
- **Background Blur** - Blur or change your background
- **Live Captions** - Automatic captions during meetings
- **Breakout Rooms** - Split into smaller groups
- **Whiteboard** - Collaborative whiteboard
- **Polls** - Create polls during meetings

## File Collaboration

## Best Practices

### Organization
- Use consistent naming for teams and channels
- Archive inactive teams
- Use channels to organize topics
- Pin important channels

### Communication
- Use @mentions sparingly
- Keep messages concise
- Use threads for discussions
- Set status and availability

### Meetings
- Schedule meetings with clear agendas
- Join on time
- Mute when not speaking
- Use chat for questions during meetings

### Security
- Review team membership regularly
- Set appropriate permissions
- Use private channels for sensitive topics
- Be cautious with external sharing
